U34CH Sound Revamp

Created In Partnership with Train Sim User Minerman, This mod completely changes the soundset of both the U34CH and the Comet I coaches. Included are also the as-intended physics and a texture fix for the trucks.


INCLUDES


A totally new soundset for both the U34CH, Comet I cab car and passenger coaches! Such as;


  • New GE 7FDL-16 prime mover audio
  • New cab sounds for both locomotive and cab car, including a conductor whistle
  • New, separate bogie sounds specific to the U34CH and Comet I coaches
  • New air brake sounds, the Comet I has accurate 'air tank' charge and discharge effects
  • New coupler audio
  • External and internal ambient audio for the coaches - AC, people talking etc.
  • Fixed horn and bell for both the cab car and locomotive


Also included are the as-intended U34CH physics and a texture patch for the trucks that fixes the missing wheel covers.


Requirements


  • NJT U34CH DLC
  • U34CH Early Years Livery Pack
  • Chessie System U30C Loco Add-On
